Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Tap "Register" on the login screen and enter your email, a display name, and a password (minimum 6 characters).

How do I join a league?

Get an invite code from your league commissioner and tap "Join League" from the home screen. Enter the code and choose a team name.

How do I reset my password?

Password reset is not yet available in-app. Please email us at mvpeav@gmail.com and we will help you reset it.

How do I delete my account?

Send a request to mvpeav@gmail.com from the email address associated with your account. We will permanently delete your data within 30 days.

Where does golf data come from?

Tournament leaderboards, player statistics, and scheduling data are provided by the Data Golf API using real PGA Tour results.

Is this app free?

Yes. Fairway Fantasy is completely free with no in-app purchases or advertisements.
